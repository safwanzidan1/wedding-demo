Our Story

How It All Began

It began with a chance encounter at a gallery opening in Florence — a shared glance over a Caravaggio painting, a borrowed umbrella in the rain, and a conversation that lasted until the last café closed its doors. Neither of us had expected to find something so quietly extraordinary in an ordinary evening.

Two years of laughter, adventures, quiet mornings, and a proposal on the shores of Lake Como later, we are ready to begin the grandest chapter of our lives — surrounded by the people we love most in the world.
