Our Story

A Love That Began
With One Glance

✦

It began with a chance encounter at a gallery opening in Florence — a shared glance over a Caravaggio painting, a borrowed umbrella in the rain, and a conversation that lasted until the last café closed its doors.

Neither of us had expected to find something so quietly extraordinary in an ordinary evening. Two years, countless adventures, and one proposal on the shores of Lake Como later, we are ready to begin our forever.

October 2021

We met at a rainy gallery opening in Florence.

June 2023

Alexander proposed at sunrise over Lake Como.

September 2025

We say "I do" — forever begins here.
